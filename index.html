Good to know

Questions,
answered.

How far in advance should I book a journey?

Most escapes are shaped 3–6 months ahead, which gives us room to secure the right suites and experiences. For peak seasons or multi-stop itineraries, 8–12 months is ideal — but we've also pulled together beautiful trips on much shorter notice.

Can my itinerary change after booking?

Yes. Your travel designer stays with you from first inquiry through your return home, and can adjust stays, pacing, or experiences as your plans evolve — subject to availability with our partner properties.

What does the estimated budget actually include?

It's a planning range covering accommodation, private transfers, and curated experiences for the group. Flights, travel insurance, and personal spending are typically quoted separately once your itinerary is confirmed.

Do you help with visas and travel documents?

We'll flag what you need and the recommended timeline for your specific itinerary and passport, and can refer you to trusted visa services — though the applications themselves are handled by you or your visa provider.

What if I need to change my travel dates?

Let your travel designer know as early as possible. Flexibility depends on each property's and partner's policies, which we'll always explain clearly before you book.
