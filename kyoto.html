Kyoto

Kyoto rewards a curious pace. Tea houses, moss gardens, and generations of craft reveal themselves in the spaces between the famous sights — usually just after sunrise, before the coach groups arrive.

Best time to go: Early-to-mid November for autumn foliage, or late March for cherry blossoms — book both far ahead.

Made for lingering

  • Private tea ceremony with a master
  • Seasonal kaiseki in Gion
  • Early morning walk through Arashiyama

Aurelia's point of view

Arashiyama's bamboo grove is stunning at 7am and a crush of shoulders by 10am. We build the whole first morning of every Kyoto itinerary around that two-hour window, then let the pace loosen for the rest of the day.
