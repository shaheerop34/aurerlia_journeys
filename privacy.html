Privacy policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Aurelia Journeys ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Inquiry details: name, email, phone, destination, travel dates, guest count, budget, travel style, and any message you submit through our planning form.
  • Newsletter signups: the email address you provide.
  • Basic technical data: standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages requested) generated automatically by visiting the site.

How we use it

We use your inquiry details solely to respond to your request and design your itinerary. We use newsletter emails only to send occasional travel ideas, and never sell or rent your information to third parties. We may share necessary details with hotel or experience partners once you've confirmed you'd like us to book on your behalf.

How long we keep it

Inquiry and booking records are kept for as long as needed to provide our services and meet our accounting and legal obligations, typically no longer than seven years. You can ask us to delete your data at any time, subject to any records we're legally required to retain.

Cookies

This site currently uses only strictly necessary, session-based technical storage — no third-party advertising or tracking cookies. If that changes, this section will be updated and, where required by law, we'll ask for your consent first.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to certain uses of it. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to hello@aureliajourneys.example.
